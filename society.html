OUR LEGACY
WHITE MEMORIAL INSTITUTIONS run under the WHITE MEMORIAL EDUCATIONAL SOCIETY, a charitable society registered under Travancore Cochin Literary & Scientific Charitable Societies Registration Act, XII/1955 as No.18 of 1974 on 11th day of Apr 1974 with Registrar of Societies - Kanyakumari District. The society is a registered public charitable society with depl of income tax vide order no 131/03-04 files C.No.464/169/2003-2004/CIT-I/MDU dated 24/01/2003.
The founder Chairman of the WHITE MEMORIAL EDUCATIONAL SOCIETY was Late. Dr. K. RAJENDRAN.M.D.
Dr. LEELA BAI RAJENDRAN, M.D. is now the Chairman of the Society. She is the treasurer of the White Memorial Educational Society and Chairman of the White Memorial

INSTITUTIONS UNDER THE SOCIETY

The following Institutions were established and run by THE WHITE MEMORIAL EDUCATIONAL SOCIETY:

The institutions are CHRISTIAN MINORITY INSTITUTIONS The FOUNDER and the MANAGEMENT are ardent members of The SEVENTH DAY ADVENTIST CHURCH.

WHITE MEMORIAL HOMOEO MEDICAL COLLEGE & HOSPITAL at Attoor-since 1980 with approval of Tamil nadu Homoeopathy Medical Council conducted DHMS course then upgraded in 1993 to BHMS degree course affiliated to TAMILNADU Dr. M.G.R MEDICAL UNIVERSITY - Chennai. In 1990, White Memorial Homoeo Medical College received permanent approval issued by Central Council of Homoeopathy - New Dehi. The college in the first private Homoeopathic Medical College in Tamil nadu. The college has the best and will experienced teaching Faculties, and also have Post Graduation Degree in Homoeopathy with 5 Specialities.

WHITE MEMORIAL COLLEGE OF PHYSIOTHERAPY at Attoor - since 2000 with the affiliation of THE Tamil nadu Dr. M.G.R Medical University, Chennai. The college has separate hostel for Boys and Girls and all other facilities, and also have Post Graduation Degree in Physiotherapy with 4 Specialities.

WHITE MEMORIAL COLLEGE OF EDUCATION at Attoor- The College was started during the academic year 2005-2008 to provide quality pedagogical education to prospective teachers. It is approved by the NCTE Bangalore and affiliated to the Manonmaniam Sundaranar University, Tinunelveli. Since 2008, the college affiliated to the Tamilnadu Teachers Education University, Chennai. The college has all the necessary facilities.

WHITE MEMORIAL COLLEGE AND SCHOOL OF NURSING at Attoor-The College was started during the academic year 2007-2008 with the affiliation of Dr. M.G.R Medical University, Chennai & NOC from Government of Tamilnadu. The College also has approval of the INC, New Delhi (File no. 02/ MAR/ 2008-INC dated 05/03/2008). The college has well experienced teaching faculties and all facilities.

WHITE MEMORIAL COLLEGE OF ARTS AND SCIENCE at Panachamoodu was started with a view to impart high standard of Education to poor students. The College was approved by the University of Kerala. All the institutions have separate hostels and all facilities like Buildings, Furniture, Exam Hall, Seminar Hall, Computer lab, Laboratory, Library, and Equipmentâ€™s etc.

WHITE MEMORIAL MATRIC & HIGHER SECONDARY SCHOOL at Marthandam- was started with a view to impart high standard of Education to poor students. The school was approved by the Dept. of School Education Govt of Tamilnadu. In this school nearby about 700 students are studying and more than 40 teaching and non teaching staffs are working. Students are admitted from LKG to SSLC.

HOSPITALS

WHITE MEMORIAL COLLEGE HOSPITAL at Attoor - is situated in the WHITE MEMORIAL INSTITUTIONS campus. The hospital is having well established Homoeopathy Unit, Modem Medicine Unit, Physiotherapy Unit and Dental Unit. The Hospital provides free medical care & Preventive Medicine for poor in rural parts of the state. The present management of the Society is very interested in expanding the hospital to Multi-Specialty Hospital.
