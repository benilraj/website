White memorial college of physiotherapy is one of the leading college for physiotherapy education in Tamilnadu. We aim to give every student in our care the very best possible education in order to prepare them for life beyond college. We encourage staff& students to involve themselves fully in all aspects of college life and to identify themselves with its core values of Respect, Responsibility, Integrity, Cooperation and Achievement. We expect high standards in all that we do at white memorial college of physiotherapy. We encourage our students to aim high in their studies, to work hard and to be sensitive to the rights and needs of others. I warmly welcome you to visit our college by contacting us, and discover firsthand what makes white memorial college of physiotherapy education so special.

AGE LIMIT

The candidate should have completed 17 years of age at the time of admission.

Eligibility Certificate:

The Candidate who has passed Higher Secondary Course Examination conducted by other than the Government of Tamil Nadu shall obtain an eligibility certificate from Tamil Nadu Dr. M.G.R. Medical University, Chennai.

Migration Certificate:

The candidates from other state or University shall obtain migration certificate from the previous University or Institution.

M.PT

MASTER OF PHYSIO THERAPY
Eligibility

Candidates Admitted into Master in Physiotherapy Course should have Passed the BPT degree Examination of the University or an examination of any other university accepted by the authority of this university as equivalent thereto. Candidates who have passed BPT Examination from other than the “The Tamil Nadu Dr. M.G.R Medical University” except Annamalai University Shall obtain an eligibility certificate from this university by remitting the prescribed fees along with the application form before seeking admission to any one of the affiliated institution.
